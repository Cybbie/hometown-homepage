Top three places to visit in Beijing

The Great Wall

Hiking tour of the Great Wall is the ultimate way to experience one of China's most famous attractions.

Temple of Heaven

One of the world's largest ceremonial complexes, and dating to the 15th century, the temple is renowned for its striking architecture, vibrant interiors, and imperial past.

Forbidden City

Forbidden City, also called The Palace Museum, is symbolic of ancient China, and is recognized as the largest wooden architectural complex in the world.
